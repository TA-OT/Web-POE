About us

History:

Ots Spark Wash is locally owned car wash that is known for its high-quality car cleaning service. Established in 2019, our business caters to individual car owners, it is in the taxi rank of Greenacres. It offers a range of car washing options including hand wash, automatic drive-through, vacuuming interior cleaning.
