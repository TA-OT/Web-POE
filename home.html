About Our Business

Ots Spark Wash is a locally owned car wash, known for its high-quality car cleaning service. Established in 2019, our business caters to individual car owners at the Greenacres Taxi Rank, Gqeberha. We offer a range of car washing options including hand wash, automatic drive-through, and vacuuming interior cleaning.

Services We Offer

  • Automatic Drive-Through
  • Vacuum Interior Cleaning
  • Waxing and Polishing
  • Engine Bay Cleaning
